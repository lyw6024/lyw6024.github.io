用Racket语言做A+B

发现北大的bailian OJ支持racket这样一种Lisp的方言，也是一门函数式的语言，稍稍学习了一点基础语法，先学读入数据和输出数据

#lang racket
(define (x)  (read-line (current-input-port)))
(define p (string-split (x) " "))
(define p1 (string->number (first p)))
(define p2 (string->number (second p)))
(printf "~a\n" (+ p1 p2))
